About me

Who am I?

My name is Dennis and I specialize in UI/UX, a student at UCL studying multimediadesign.

In the fast-paced world of web-development and design, I plan to stand as a dedicated professional, pushing the borders of technology to deliver visually intriguing and memorable experiences.

As a student at UCL I know how to stay up to date and on top of every new trend whether it's the newest Adobe program, popular game release or an upcoming concert. I'll most definitely know what the people want to see, which means I will act as their guide on your website.

What have I done so far?

During my time as a designer I've gathered and collected numerous invaluable data. Specifically on the process of design, I have gotten proficient and quick when it comes to producing what both the client and users want alike.
