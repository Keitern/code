Re-design of Odense Skakklub

Wireframing, prototyping, user testing.

In this project the goal was to find a new look for Odense Skakklub, in this case it was a complete re-design of their original site, which in essence means there's a lot of steps involved.

Namely moodboarding has to be taken into consideration, same goes for multiple style tiles, different prototypes and iterations as well as user testing to base those different iterations and the whole re-design upon.

In terms of this project the focus had primarily been on creating prototypes of our new design, and secondly user testing. Through serious thought and use of design thinking, prototyping had been done upon thorough research of user behaviour and preferences.

Through this project I've gathered and collected invaluable information and experience, giving me the abilities to accurately and precisely figure out what the user wants, aswell as what is needed for a website to function optimally.
