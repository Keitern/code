Girl with the pearl earring: Animation

For this project the goal was to successfully animate a painting, making it look more lifelike this was done using Adobe Photoshop, aswell as Adobe After effects. What I did was cutting out the eyes, earring and bringing in a hand in photoshop, whereafter I did some editing in After effects, creating the effect of her dangling her earring aswell as blinking at the viewer.
